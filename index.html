Executive Summary

ABQ Street Art is a mobile-first website that allows users to explore public art in Downtown Albuquerque. By integrating with the Google Maps API and pulling information about art pieces from the Public Art Database, users will be able to learn more about the art and its artist, favorite locations, and opt-in to receive notifications when they are near a piece of public art.

Key Features

  • Mobile-first web application used by art enthusiasts of all levels.
  • Focuses on specific section of Downtown Albuquerque.
  • Base interface features a map of public art locations marked with pins. Pins are clickable and provide more information: photo, artist, date created, etc.
  • Users are able to bookmark/favorite pieces of art.
  • Users are able to "opt-in" to receive notifications when they are nearby to art that they have expressed interest in.
  • We are hoping to include 1-2 predetermined/suggested routes that users can utilize (but not create).
