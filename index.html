Executive Summary

  • Mobile-first web application used by art enthusiasts of levels.
  • Focuses on specific section of Downtown Albuquerque.
  • Base interface features a map of public art locations marked with pins. Pins are clickable and provide more information: photo, artist, date created, etc.
  • Users are able to bookmark/favorite pieces of art.
  • Users are able to "opt-in" to receive notifications when they are nearby to art that they have expressed interest in.
  • We are hoping to include 1-2 predetermined/suggested routes that users can utilize (but not create).
